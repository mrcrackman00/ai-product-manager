✦ Our Story

The problem we lived ourselves

In early 2024, our founding team was building a B2B SaaS tool. We did everything right — 30+ user interviews, detailed notes, thematic analysis. But after a two-week sprint, we shipped a feature that nobody used. We'd been optimizing for the loudest voices, not the most common pain points.

We realized the problem wasn't the interviews — it was the analysis. Manual thematic coding takes hours and introduces bias. Senior PMs do it intuitively after years of practice. But what about the indie hacker, the first-time founder, the product manager at their second job with 15 Slack threads to catch up on?

We built ProdMind to solve this. We embedded the Mom Test, Jobs-to-be-Done theory, and YC's product playbook directly into our AI engine — so anyone can get senior PM quality analysis in seconds, not hours.

We're a small, remote team that ships fast and talks to our users every week. (Yes, we eat our own dogfood.)

✦ What We Believe

Our values
